The art of creating the look of stone, brick and marble is an advanced faux technique.  Creating walls to look like brick or stone is one of Ron's greatest talent.  (Be sure to check out the "Trompe L'oeil" section to see his expert Brick Motifs.  He is an expert at matching existing brick or stone from the interior or exterior of the client's home and has the ability to match it so well in a photo you can not tell the difference from the original real material and Ron's painted version.  Marbleizing is an excellent technique to use on columns, counter tops, tables and concrete floors to create the expensive look of real marble.  Any of the samples below of Ron's various stones, bricks and marble can be used on concrete floors.  Ron can also create the look of a wood floor.

Merlin's Chess

This is the first public exposition of the magical chess game of Gwyddbwyll, as mentioned in the Mabinogion and the Welsh Triads. The weekend will consist of  lectures, hands on instruction,  pathworking and  ritual . Some knowledge of the Mabinogion would be useful. TEMPLE  SKILLS

BOOKINGS NOW CLOSED

Traditional temple skills are normally only taught to initiates within closed magical orders. This weekend will teach you how to: Compose and perform ritual, how to structure effective power flows, the magical uses of colour, movement, sound and smell. It will also give an introduction to the techniques of Priest and Priestess mediation and polarity working. Many people perform  individual and  group ceremonies in the belief that such rituals  are effectual. But effective ritual depends upon a precise series of specially focused and executed  techniques,  which will be explained and experienced this weekend.  Robes and sense of humour are especially required for this one!

Isis and Osiris.

Your Tutors

Mike Harris

Mike Harris has worked in the Western Mystery tradition for longer than he cares to remember. He was trained by Gareth Knight and thus traces his magical lineage to Dion Fortune and before that to the Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn. He was one of the founding Magi of the Gareth Knight Group in the mid 1980's and directed that group, overseeing its transformation to the Avalon Group in 1999. He is presently Magus of the Company of Avalon.  Mike has taught workshops and promoted the Western Mystery Tradition, particularly its Celtic aspect, in the UK and USA for a number of years. He has appeared on British television and written countless articles. He is author of three books and is a published poet. He has lived in North Wales for over thirty years in the atmospheric mountain scenery of Taliesin and the Mabinogion and is on the advisory board of the Wisdom of the Ancients project. 
Naomi Ozaniec

Naomi Ozaniec has worked in the Western Mystery Tradition since 1975 as a writer, teacher and explorer and facilitator of most of the aspects of that tradition. She has written more than a dozen books, giving practical instruction on, among other subjects, the Tarot, The Tree of Life, the Chakras, dowsing, and meditation. Titles include Daughters of the Goddess and the Aquarian Qabalah, a complete mystery course text on the Tree of Life and the Tarot. 

Naomi has worked with leading pioneers in the Tradition, including Gareth Knight, Dolores Ashcroft-Nowicki and Marian Green. She served as the oracular voice of the Isian Assembly at the World Parliament of Religions in Chicago in 1993. She is director of the House of Life Mystery School.
